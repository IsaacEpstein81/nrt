Three Outcomes

A single platform that helps staff learn, practice, ask, and improve.

The Phase 1 engagement centers on three tightly connected outcomes that improve frontline readiness while giving NRT leadership better coaching visibility.

Outcome A AI Learning & Simulation Platform

NRT gets a structured training environment inside OpenRecovery’s LMS where staff move beyond passive modules and actively apply what they learn.

  • Interactive modules aligned to NRT training priorities
  • AI roleplay exercises, quizzes, scoring, and immediate feedback
  • Practice before real-world application and better measurement of readiness

Outcome B NRT Organizational Copilot

Staff gain faster, more consistent access to approved guidance instead of relying on static documents and repeated supervisor clarification.

  • Grounded in approved NRT materials, training content, and internal guidance
  • Supports questions during training, preparation, and day-to-day reinforcement
  • Scales into a durable knowledge layer as NRT adds new use cases

Outcome C Supervisor Visibility & Analytics

Leadership gains a clearer line of sight into learner progress, repeated question themes, and where added coaching or content refinement is needed.

  • Progress and completion visibility across staff and locations
  • Analytics on common struggle points and repeated support themes
  • A feedback loop for targeted coaching and future training improvements

Phase 1 Activation

Phase 1 stands up the initial NRT training environment and prepares it for launch.

The objective is to deploy NRT’s first AI-supported training environment using the OpenRecovery LMS, NRT Copilot, roleplay workflows, and reporting structure.

01
Collect and prioritize

Artifact collection, document review, and alignment on the first training topics to activate.

02
Ground and configure

Copilot setup, NRT grounding, roleplay configuration, and quiz or scoring setup based on the selected path.

03
Review and test

Supervisor visibility setup, stakeholder review, testing, and refinement before launch readiness.

04
Launch and expand

Deliver a live NRT training environment, then use Phase 2 to add locations, topics, reporting, and new workflow use cases.

Phase 1 Deliverable

A live NRT training environment that includes interactive learning, Copilot support, and supervisor visibility, with the depth of customization determined by the selected deployment path.

Core Workstreams

  • AI Learning & Simulation Platform
  • Organizational Knowledge Copilot
  • Supervisor Visibility & Training Analytics

Phase 2 Expansion

Phase 2 can extend the foundation across additional locations, more training topics, broader reporting, and additional roleplay or workflow use cases.

Scope, Delivery & Acceptance

Phase 1 stays focused on a launch-ready deployment without overpromising custom infrastructure.

Deliverables May Include

  • An NRT-configured AI Learning & Simulation Platform
  • An NRT-trained Organizational Knowledge Copilot
  • Initial NRT-specific roleplay scenarios
  • Supervisor visibility into progress, engagement, and common support needs
  • A launch-ready deployment plan for broader rollout

Acceptance Criteria

  • Functional deployment of the agreed Phase 1 system
  • Leadership validation of usability and fit
  • Completion of the agreed implementation scope

Phase 1 Exclusions

  • Deep EMR or third-party system integrations
  • Unlimited content conversion
  • Enterprise-wide workflow redesign
  • Client-facing digital care deployment
  • Custom credentialing or CE infrastructure
  • Broader operational infrastructure outside the selected implementation path
